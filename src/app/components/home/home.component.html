SCRIPTING SEIGE

Program Your units.
Win the war.


Scripting Siege is a free educational game for both beginner and intermediate programmers

Learn to Code. For Free



Scripting siege is a free educational game for both beginner and intermediate programmers.

Choose between easy to read blockcode or program your units with javascript.

OUR MISSION

Learn

Understand the basics of JavaScript with Scripting seige!

Practice

Many levels to play with!

Improve

As your characters get smarter, so do you!
